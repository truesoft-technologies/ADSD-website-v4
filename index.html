Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner in Steel Engineering

ADSD Steel Technical Services Contracting L.L.C is a Dubai-based industrial contracting company with over two decades of proven expertise in the steel fabrication and construction sector. Our extensive experience in the industry has been fundamental in securing work from major corporate companies across the UAE.

What We Do

Our Core Expertise

Extensive experience in the industry has been fundamental in securing work from corporate companies. We have segmented our main objectives into six core categories.

01

Structural Steel Fabrication & Installation

Precision fabrication and on-site installation of structural steel frameworks for industrial, commercial, and utility facilities across the UAE.

02

Equipment Installation

Professional installation of heavy industrial equipment and machinery, ensuring safe integration within structural environments.

03

Pipe Fabrication, Installation & Tanks

Full-scope pipe fabrication, inline equipment installation, and tanks installation for industrial and process plant environments.

04

Miscellaneous Metal Work

Custom metalwork including handrails, aluminium louvers, car parking sheds, substation chequer plates, and bespoke fabrication.

05

Manpower Supply

Skilled and semi-skilled manpower supply for construction and industrial projects, with trained personnel ready for immediate deployment.

06

Anchor Bolts & Fasteners Supply

Reliable supply of anchor bolts, fasteners, and structural fixings to meet the precise specifications of your construction projects.

Supply Service

Committed to supplying exceptional quality products and services in the pre-fabricated construction industry by developing and employing the most advanced information, engineering, manufacturing, and delivery systems available. Contact our friendly sales staff for a free quote via phone, fax, or email.

Building Maintenance

Our in-house staff performs all cleaning and maintenance, trash and recycling management, and handyman work. Our trained property managers schedule and monitor all work we perform. We are always fully accountable for these services, as our own team carries out the work — without outsourcing or sub-contracting.
